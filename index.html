What We Offer

Comprehensive Medical Services

BRISK Hospital offers a full spectrum of healthcare services delivered by highly trained specialists using modern equipment.

Cardiology

Advanced cardiac care including echocardiography, stress tests, angioplasty, and heart surgeries by top cardiologists.

Neurology

Expert diagnosis and treatment of stroke, epilepsy, Parkinson's disease, migraines, and neurological disorders.

Orthopaedics

Comprehensive bone, joint, and muscle care including arthroscopy, joint replacement, and sports injury rehabilitation.

Maternity & NICU

Safe, supportive maternal care from prenatal to postnatal, with a fully equipped Neonatal Intensive Care Unit.

Laboratory & Diagnostics

State-of-the-art lab services including MRI, CT scans, X-ray, ultrasound, and comprehensive blood panels.

Pharmacy

24-hour in-house pharmacy with a wide range of medications, supplements, and patient counselling services.

Ophthalmology

Complete eye care including cataract surgery, glaucoma treatment, laser vision correction, and optical services.

Emergency & Trauma

24/7 emergency services with a dedicated trauma team, ambulance fleet, and rapid triage system.
